Our Farming Legacy

For many years, AncestTree AgriInvest has passionately cultivated the rich, fertile soils of Ghana, transforming them into thriving farmlands that produce high-quality, sustainable crops and well-nurtured livestock. Our commitment goes beyond agriculture. we are dedicated to empowering local communities, preserving the environment, and delivering consistent value to our investors. By blending traditional farming wisdom with modern, eco-friendly practices, we’re not just growing food, we’re growing a future you can believe in and invest in with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about investing with AncesTree AgrInvest.

After registering and selecting your preferred farming package, you'll make your investment payment. We then allocate your funds to the specific farming activity you've chosen. Throughout the farming cycle, you'll receive regular updates on your investment's progress. At harvest time, we sell the produce and distribute your principal plus the agreed returns.

We have multiple risk mitigation strategies in place including crop insurance, diversified farming across different locations, and modern farming techniques that reduce the chance of total loss. In the rare event of significant loss, our insurance coverage helps protect investors' capital.

Update frequency depends on the package you choose. Maize and tomato investors receive weekly updates, poultry investors get updates every two weeks, and goat investors receive monthly updates. All investors also get special updates for major milestones like planting, growth stages, and harvest.

Absolutely! We organize quarterly farm visits for our investors. These visits allow you to see firsthand how your investment is being managed, meet our farming team, and learn more about our operations. You'll receive notifications about upcoming visit opportunities through your investor dashboard.

Returns are paid directly to your registered bank account or mobile money wallet at the end of each farming cycle. You'll receive a detailed statement showing your principal investment and the returns earned. Most investors receive their payments within 14 days after harvest and sales are completed.
